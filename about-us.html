ABOUT COMPANY

Established in the year 1995 by Mehboob Ali Shaikh. Anmol interiors & architects is named after completing our very first project at Anmol complex, Andheri. With more than 25 years of working span we have successfully accomplished more than 100 projects in Mumbai and other regions. Initially it was an interior designing firm but recently we have added a new dimension of architecture and planning to it.
